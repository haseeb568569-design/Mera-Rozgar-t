Frequently asked

Do I have to pay anything to join?
No. Signing up and earning is completely free. There is no package to purchase and no deposit required at any point.
Why do other apps pay so much more per ad?
If a payout is far above what advertisers actually pay for an ad view, it isn't coming from ad revenue — it's coming from other users' deposits. We only pay out what advertisers actually pay us.
Do I need referrals to withdraw?
No. Referrals are optional and give a small bonus, but they are never required to withdraw your own earnings.
How fast are withdrawals processed?
Most withdrawals to JazzCash or Easypaisa are reviewed and sent within 24 hours once you're above the Rs 150 minimum.
